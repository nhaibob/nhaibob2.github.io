Dune

"Dune" by Frank Herbert is an iconic science fiction novel set on the desert planet Arrakis, where noble families vie for control of the valuable spice melange. Young Paul Atreides must navigate treacherous politics and confront his hidden powers as he becomes a pivotal figure in the fate of Arrakis and the universe. Herbert's visionary world-building and compelling characters make "Dune" a timeless masterpiece of science fiction literature.

How to win Friends and Influence people

"How to Win Friends and Influence People" by Dale Carnegie is a timeless self-help classic that offers practical and effective advice on building meaningful relationships and improving communication skills. With practical techniques and real-life examples, Carnegie guides readers on how to handle people, make them feel appreciated, and become influential in personal and professional settings. This influential book has empowered countless individuals to enhance their social interactions, gain confidence, and achieve success by mastering the art of human connection.

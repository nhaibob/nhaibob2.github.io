In The Woods

"In the Woods" by Tana French is a gripping mystery novel that follows Detective Rob Ryan as he investigates a disturbing murder in a small Irish town. Set against the haunting backdrop of an old forest, the case stirs up buried memories from Ryan's past when he was the sole survivor of a childhood tragedy in the same woods. As he delves deeper into the present-day murder and his own past, secrets unravel, and the haunting suspense keeps readers on the edge of their seats until the shocking conclusion. French's masterful storytelling and rich character development make "In the Woods" a captivating and atmospheric thriller.

The Glass Castle

"The Glass Castle" by Jeannette Walls is a compelling memoir that recounts her tumultuous upbringing with unconventional parents who lead a nomadic and impoverished lifestyle. Walls vividly depicts her journey from a childhood marked by neglect and hardship to finding her own identity as a successful journalist. Through candid storytelling, "The Glass Castle" explores themes of resilience, family bonds, and the pursuit of a better life despite adversity, leaving readers both moved and inspired.
